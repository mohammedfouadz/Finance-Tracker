PART 1 — HAWL SETUP (in Step 1 of Zakat wizard)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Replace the current simple "Hawl Confirmed" toggle with a rich setup card:

CARD: "Set Your Zakat Anniversary Date"
Subtitle: "Choose when to calculate and pay your Zakat each year"

Two clear options presented as radio cards:

OPTION A (recommended): "Fixed Annual Date" 
- Large visual card with moon/calendar icon
- Description: "Choose a memorable Islamic date to calculate Zakat every 
  year. Most practical for regular Muslims."
- Date picker with Hijri calendar:
  - Dropdown suggestions: "1 Ramadan", "1 Muharram", "15 Shaban", 
    "Day of Arafah", "1 Dhul-Hijjah", "Custom date"
  - Custom date: full Hijri date picker with Gregorian equivalent shown
- Preview: "You'll pay Zakat every year on 1 Ramadan (≈ March 2, 2026 
  this year)"

OPTION B: "Track My Nisab Date Precisely"
- Card with scales icon
- Description: "I know the exact date my wealth first exceeded Nisab. 
  Calculate based on 354-day lunar cycle."
- Date picker: "When did your wealth first exceed Nisab?"
  - Both Hijri and Gregorian date pickers
  - "I'm not sure" option → falls back to Option A with education
- Preview: "Your Hawl completes on [date] · Zakat due: [amount]"

Behavior:
- Once set, the date is saved to user profile
- Can be changed anytime from Settings → Zakat Preferences
- First-time setup triggers onboarding modal explaining options
